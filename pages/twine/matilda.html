Matilda

Description

During October 2023, me and Qizilan were given two weeks to make a narrative branching game with Twine. This current version of Matilda has seven different endings. My contributions included idea generation, HTML & JavaScript programming, passage implementation, music SFX editing, and video editing. Matilda can be played in English, and Simplified Chinese.
