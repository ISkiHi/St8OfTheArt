MSA

Description

During February 2023, I was tasked to make my FMP based on previous research and planning back in September 2022. MSA is a first person game. Each room requires a terminal, and a document(s) to find. Some of the documents will contain a password which the player can type into the terminal. Assuming the password is correct, a teleporter will open. When the player walks through the teleporter, the teleporter will teleport the player to the next room. The final rooms terminal will open the locked entrance door in the first room. The player will have 10 minutes to leave the facility. Also, this is the first game I decided to experiment with AI art. I made this game in Unreal Engine 5 and finished the game during May 2023.
