Nom Nom Fish Wars

Description

During November 2023, I was given two weeks to make a board or card game that can fit into a small box. Nom Nom Fish Wars is a two-player board game, and the first player to capture three flags win. This game contains a board, five centimetre cubes, A-6 cards (4x), shark cards(4x), and a ruleset.
