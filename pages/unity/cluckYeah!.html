Cluck Yeah!

Description

A score-based Hybrid-Casual arcade infinite vertical scroller made by Gamecrafters' Collective using Unity. Players tap to bounce a chicken upwards, avoiding semi-procedurally generated (RNG) hazards while completing goals/milestones. My contributions include obstacle collisions, and managing obstacle and asset spawning during level transitions (not yet pushed on development build). Cluck Yeah! is currently in public beta stage and will be continuously updated over time.
