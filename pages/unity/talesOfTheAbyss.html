Tales of the Abyss

Description

Tales of the Abyss is a 2D adventure game, made for a UNESCO brief. The game starts in the deep ocean, and explore how humans impacted the oceans environment for water life. The player must collect the spirit within each level to move on and get to the ocean shore. *FIRST LOAD MAY TAKE LONGER TO LOAD
