Order in Chaos

Description

*OCULUS VR HEADSET REQUIRED

Order in Chaos is a 3D Virtual Reality game, where players start off in their own apartment, realising that the chaos and mess within their studio is giving them a strange feeling of anxiety. Slowly, players decide to start tidying up and organise their room, closet, and bathroom by following their own to-do list. However, they only have limited time to pull themselves together and create order in a chaotic room.
